\subsection{D≈Økaz souvislosti minima a maxima}

Tvrzen√≠. Pro $f: D \rightarrow \R, M \subseteq D, \hat{x} \in M$ plat√≠:

\begin{enumerate}[(1)]
    \item $\hat{x} \in \underset{x \in M}{\argmin} f(x) \iff \hat{x} \in \underset{x \in M}{\argmax} (-f(x))$,
    \item jesli≈æe $\hat{x} \in \underset{x \in M}{\argmin} f(x)$, pak $\underset{x \in M}{\min} f(x) =
    - \underset{x \in M}{\max} (-f(x))$.
\end{enumerate}
D≈Økaz.

(1) $\hat{x} \in \underset{x \in M}{\argmin} f(x)$, tj. $f(\hat{x}) \leq f(x), \forall x \in M \underset{\cdot (-1)}{\iff}
-f(\hat{x}) \geq -f(x), \forall x \in M$, tj. $\hat{x} \in \underset{x \in M}{\argmax} (-f(x)). \qed$


(2) A≈• $\hat{x} \in \underset{x \in M}{\argmin} f(x)$, pak $\underset{x \in M}{\min} f(x) = f(\hat{x}) =
- (- f(\hat{x})) \overset{(1)}{=} - \underset{x \in M}{\max} (-f(x)). \qed$

\section*{Konvexn√≠ mno≈æiny} \label{sec:konvex}
Definice. Mno≈æina $C \subseteq \R^n$ se nazve konvexn√≠, jestli≈æe pro ka≈æd√© $x, y \in C$ je $[x,y] \in C$.

\subsection{Uzav≈ôen√° √∫seƒçka}
Nech≈• $x, y \in \R^n$. Mno≈æina
\[ [x,y] \coloneq \bc{\lambda x + (1-\lambda)y \mid 0 \leq \lambda \leq 1} \]
se naz√Ωv√° uzav≈ôen√° √∫seƒçka s krajn√≠mi body $x$ a $y$.

\subsection{Je nadrovina konvexn√≠?}
Definice nadroviny: $H(y; \alpha) \coloneq \bc{x \in \R^n \mid \langle x, y \rangle = \alpha}$, $y \in \R^n$, $\alpha \in \R$.

D≈Økaz.

A≈• $x,z \in H(y, \alpha), \lambda \in [0,1]$.\\
C√≠l: $\lambda x + (1-\lambda) z \in H(y, \alpha)$. Tedy dokazujeme podle \hyperref[sec:konvex]{definice}.

$\langle \lambda x + (1-\lambda)z, y \rangle = \lambda \underbrace{\langle x,y \rangle}_{\alpha} + (1-\lambda)
\underbrace{\langle z,y \rangle}_{\alpha} = \lambda \alpha + (1-\lambda) \alpha = \alpha$.

$\Rightarrow \lambda x + (1-\lambda)z \in H(y, \alpha). \qed$

\subsection{Je uzav≈ôen√Ω poloprostor konvexn√≠?}

\subsection{Je uzav≈ôen√° koule konvexn√≠?}
Definice uzav≈ôen√© koule: $B(a,r) = \bc{a \in \R^n \mid ||x -a || \leq r}$, o st≈ôedu $a \in \R^n$ a polomƒõru $r > 0$.

D≈Økaz.

A≈• $x,y \in \R^n, \lambda \in [0,1]$.\\
C√≠l: $|| [\lambda x + (1-\alpha)y] - a || \leq r$. Tedy za $x$ z definice dosad√≠me √∫seƒçku mezi body $x$ a $y$, kter√© jsme
si vybrali a chceme uk√°zat, ≈æe i tato √∫seƒçka le≈æ√≠ v uzav≈ôen√© kouli, dle \hyperref[sec:konvex]{definice}.

\[
    || [\lambda x + (1-\alpha)y] - a || = || \lambda x - (1-\lambda)a + (1-\lambda)y - \lambda a || =
    || \lambda (x-a) + (1-\lambda)(y-a) ||
\]
\[
    \leq \lambda ||\underbrace{x-a}_{\leq r}|| +  (1-\lambda)||\underbrace{y-a}_{\leq r}|| \leq \lambda r + (1-\lambda)r
     = r. \qed
\]

\subsection{Je okol√≠ konvexn√≠?}
Definice okol√≠: $B(a,r) = \bc{a \in \R^n \mid ||x -a || < r}$, o st≈ôedu $a \in \R^n$ a polomƒõru $r > 0$.

D≈Økaz.

A≈• $x,y \in \R^n, \lambda \in [0,1]$.\\
C√≠l: $|| [\lambda x + (1-\alpha)y] - a || < r$. Dle \hyperref[sec:konvex]{definice}.

\[

\section*{Afinn√≠ zobrazen√≠} \label{sec:afin}
Definice. Zobrazen√≠ $f: \R^n \rightarrow \R^m$ se naz√Ωv√° afinn√≠, existuj√≠-li $A \in \M_{m,n} (\R)$ a $b \in R^m$

\subsection{D≈Økaz, ≈æe afinn√≠ zobrazen√≠ je konvexn√≠}
Tvrzen√≠.

Nech≈• $f: \R^n \rightarrow \R^m$. Pak $f$ je \hyperref[sec:afin]{afinn√≠} $\iff$ pro ka≈æd√© $x,y \in \R^n$ a ka≈æd√©
$\lambda \in \R$ plat√≠
\[f(\lambda x + (1-\lambda) y) =\lambda f(x) + (1-\lambda) f(y)\text{.}\]

D≈Økaz.

\enquote{$\Rightarrow$}: A≈• $f(x) = Ax + b$, kde $A \in \M_{m,n} (\R)$, $b \in \R^n$.

A≈• $x, y \in \R^n, \lambda \in \R$.
\[
    f(\lambda x + (1 - \lambda) y) = A [\lambda x + (1-\lambda) y] + b = \lambda A x + (1-\lambda)Ay + \lambda b +
    (1-\lambda)b =
\]
\[
    \lambda \underbrace{(Ax + b)}_{f(x)} + (1-\lambda)\underbrace{(Ay + b)}_{f(y)} = \lambda f(x) + (1-\lambda)f(y). \qed
\]

\enquote{$\Leftarrow$}:
C√≠l: Uk√°zat, ≈æe $f$ je \hyperref[sec:afin]{afinn√≠}, tedy $f(x) = Ax + b$.\\
Zvolme $\varphi(x) = f(x) - f(0)$.\\
Pokud je $f$ \hyperref[sec:afin]{afinn√≠}, pak zobrazen√≠ $\varphi$ by mƒõlo b√Ωt d√°no jako $Ax$, tedy b√Ωt line√°rn√≠.\\
C√≠l: $\varphi$ je line√°rn√≠ zobrazen√≠.

Mus√≠me ovƒõ≈ôit uzav≈ôenost na n√°soben√≠ a sƒç√≠t√°n√≠ z definice.

(1) A≈• $x \in \R^n$, $\alpha \in R$.\\
C√≠l: $\varphi(\alpha x) = \alpha \varphi(x)$.
\[
    \varphi(\alpha x) = f(\alpha x) - f(0) = f(\alpha x + (1-\alpha)0) - f(0) = \alpha f(x) + (1-\alpha)f(0) - f(0) =
\]
\[
    \alpha f(x) - \alpha f(0) = \alpha (f(x) - f(0)) = \alpha \varphi (x-0). \qed
\]

(2) A≈• $x, y \in \R^n$.\\
C√≠l: $\varphi(x+y) = \varphi(x) + \varphi(y)$.
\[
    \varphi(x+y) = \varphi \left(2 \left(\frac{1}{2} (x+y)\right)\right) \stackrel{(1)}{=} 2 \varphi \left(\frac{1}{2} (x+y)\right) =
    2 \left[f(\frac{1}{2}x + \frac{1}{2}y) - f(0)\right] = 2 \left[\frac{1}{2} f(x) + \frac{1}{2}f(y) - f(0) \right] =
\]
\[
    f(x) + f(y) - f(0) - f(0) = \underbrace{f(x) - f(0)}_{\varphi(x)} + \underbrace{f(y) - f(0)}_{\varphi(y)} =
    \varphi(x) + \varphi(y). \qed
\]
